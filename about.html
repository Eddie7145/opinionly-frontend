Upvote

The term "Upvote" is used as a benchmark to help analyze and know how many people agree with a certain opinion. Opinions with higher upvotes are suggested to new users with similar questions, since that opinion is agreed upon by the majority. Opinions with low downvotes have high upvotes and are suggested more to people with similar questions.

Downvote

The term "Downvote" is used as a benchmark to help analyze and know how many people disagree with a certain opinion. Opinions with higher downvotes are least suggested as a large amount of people disagree. Opinions with low downvotes have high upvotes and are suggested more to people with similar questions.
